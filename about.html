Who We Are

Sewa.com is a trusted in-home care service provider in Nepal, built to support families living abroad who worry about their elderly loved ones back home. With a passionate team of caregivers, nurses, and support staff, we deliver high-quality care that ensures health, happiness, and dignity for aging parents in their own homes.

Why Choose Us?

  • Compassionate, culturally sensitive caregivers
  • Personalized care plans tailored to each family's needs
  • Health monitoring, companionship, and emotional support
  • Daily updates via phone, messages, or video calls
  • Affordable and transparent pricing

What We Offer

  • Personal Care: Bathing, grooming, hygiene assistance
  • Companionship: Conversations, daily activities, mental stimulation
  • Medical Care: Medication management, check-ups, skilled nursing
  • Household Help: Cooking, cleaning, shopping, and more
  • Memory Care: Dementia and Alzheimer's support
  • Emergency Assistance: 24/7 access and response team
